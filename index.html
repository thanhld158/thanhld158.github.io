About

I am an independent and experience iOS Engineer with many high quality applications released during 5+ years working in Software Development. Having done more than 20 projects from vary range of domains, include these amazing apps YLNews, FailED, Golden, Coinleap, I strongly believe in developing your idea to next level. My full resume can be found at LinkedIn.

Cycling, playing guitar, reading startup book or browsing Kickstarter are those my hobbies when not coding.

Do you need a professional and dedicated iOS developer for your next project? I am here, FOR HIRE. I have built apps for both companies and my own business so that proven skills and quality. Not only coding, but also consulting and sharing business mind.

High responsibility, proactive, awareness of time, budget, quality and always keen on incredible ideas, I'm willing to take all talent to bring the world fantasy applications from your idea. Just drop me an email and start the next game changer.
